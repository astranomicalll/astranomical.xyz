Akivili

The Trailblaze Aeon — Deceased

The Aeon of Trailblaze, whose nature embodied the boundless pursuit of the unknown. Unlike other members who hold their genius through mortal intellect, Akivili's genius was cosmological — the living embodiment of curiosity and forward motion. Now gone, their path endures in those who follow the stars.
